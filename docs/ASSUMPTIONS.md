# Assumptions and decision log

These decisions resolve ambiguity in the product brief. They are implementation constraints, not medical or legal advice.

| ID    | Decision                                                                                                                                                                                                                     | Rationale / consequence                                                                                                                                  |
| ----- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| A-001 | DENTAL TRUST is a coordination and evidence-verification platform, not a healthcare provider.                                                                                                                                | Clinical decisions, diagnoses, informed consent, and treatment remain with licensed providers and patients. UI copy must not imply guarantees.           |
| A-002 | English and Vietnamese are equally supported product locales; English is the fallback for missing operational metadata only.                                                                                                 | User-visible release content cannot ship with fallback keys or mixed-language placeholders.                                                              |
| A-003 | Dates are stored as UTC instants and rendered with an explicit IANA timezone. Clinic availability defaults to `Asia/Ho_Chi_Minh`; patient views default to their saved timezone.                                             | Prevents cross-border scheduling ambiguity and daylight-saving errors.                                                                                   |
| A-004 | Monetary amounts are stored in integer minor units with an ISO-4217 currency. Quotes may show VND and USD, but accepted plan/payment versions preserve the original amount and exchange-rate disclosure.                     | Avoids floating-point errors and silent repricing.                                                                                                       |
| A-005 | A user may belong to multiple organizations, but each privileged request operates in one explicit organization/tenant scope.                                                                                                 | Role alone is never sufficient; membership and resource assignment are checked per request.                                                              |
| A-006 | Caregiver access is opt-in, granular, revocable, and time-bounded where the patient chooses an expiry.                                                                                                                       | Revocation invalidates new access immediately and is audit logged; historical audit events remain.                                                       |
| A-007 | Verification is evidence-based, time-bounded, and non-purchasable. Expired, revoked, or incomplete evidence removes public verified eligibility.                                                                             | Prevents advertising spend from affecting verification or ranking.                                                                                       |
| A-008 | Production files are private by default and are accessed only through short-lived signed URLs after server-side authorization.                                                                                               | Object keys are not treated as authorization and are never public URLs.                                                                                  |
| A-009 | Stripe is the initial payment adapter. Mailpit and test-mode Stripe are development/test facilities only.                                                                                                                    | Production startup or use of a provider-backed flow fails safely when live configuration is absent.                                                      |
| A-010 | PostgreSQL is the source of truth; Redis/BullMQ holds ephemeral queue coordination, not irreplaceable clinical state.                                                                                                        | Critical job intent uses a transactional outbox before asynchronous delivery.                                                                            |
| A-011 | Soft deletion is used only where business history or legal retention requires it; privacy erasure uses irreversible anonymization or deletion according to the approved retention policy.                                    | “Soft delete everything” is not treated as compliance.                                                                                                   |
| A-012 | The initial production region and legal retention periods are deployment decisions requiring counsel and a data-processing review.                                                                                           | No fixed regulatory retention period is invented; see known limitations and privacy architecture.                                                        |
| A-013 | Development seed identities use a clearly documented shared local password and are prohibited when `NODE_ENV=production`.                                                                                                    | Enables deterministic demos/tests without creating a production backdoor.                                                                                |
| A-014 | ClamAV is the local malware scanner. Production may use ClamAV or a managed equivalent behind the same fail-closed adapter.                                                                                                  | Uploaded objects remain quarantined until the scanner produces a clean verdict.                                                                          |
| A-015 | SMS and consumer messaging channels are optional notification adapters, not authoritative medical channels.                                                                                                                  | Sensitive details are minimized; in-app secure messaging remains the record of coordination.                                                             |
| A-016 | An external calendar synchronization service owns provider OAuth credentials and raw provider calendar identifiers. Dental Trust addresses it with an opaque connection ID and stores only a hash of the external reference. | Limits provider-secret exposure; external busy windows are not treated as scheduling evidence until an approved import/reconciliation contract exists.   |
| A-017 | The standalone Provider product is the canonical clinic/dentist daily workspace; legacy clinic routes in the shared web portal remain compatibility surfaces while workflows are migrated.                                   | Provider may reuse shared contracts and APIs, but it owns its navigation, responsive behavior, BFF allowlist, and clinical-workspace interaction design. |

## Decisions still requiring production owners

- Hosting region, recovery objectives, and approved cross-border transfer mechanism.
- Legal retention schedule by record category and jurisdiction.
- Production payment, email, telemetry, error-tracking, SMS, video-consultation, and object-storage accounts.
- Formal verification evidence checklist and reviewer accreditation policy approved by compliance counsel.
